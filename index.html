About Me

I am a pragmatic team player who contributes skills in a range of professional areas, with special emphasis on public engagement, project and program management, planning, research, analysis, and environmental and recreational education. I am a strong communicator, both orally and in writing, learn and adapt quickly, and enjoy challenging work. As an innovative problem solver, I am results oriented and simultaneously demonstrate flexibility, creativity, and perceptiveness.

I have a M.A. in Cultural Anthropology and a B.A. in Anthropology with a Minor in History from George Mason University.

I currently serve the citizens of Hillsborough County, FL as a Senior Park Ranger at Edward Medard Conservation Park.
